About us

Welcome to Rhythm Roulette, a groundbreaking platform where your musical journey is crafted uniquely for you.

In a world overwhelmed with endless playlists and overwhelming choices, we asked ourselves a simple yet profound question: "What song should I listen to next?" From this curiosity, our team embarked on a mission to redefine how you discover music. Our process begins with you. By answering a few simple survey questions, you open a window into your musical soul. We’ve designed an intuitive, JavaScript-powered survey that captures your preferences, moods, and genres.

This interactive experience allows us to understand your musical landscape deeply. Rhythm Roulette is more than just a recommendation engine; it's a companion in your musical exploration. We invite you to dive into a world where your next favorite song is just a few clicks away, tailored precisely to your taste and mood.
